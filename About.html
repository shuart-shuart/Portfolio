Hong Shu-ying (b.1997, Singapore) negotiates different coping methods to deal with loss, absences, and revelations. She seeks to better understand the relationship between people, time, and space. Her works span mediums like photography and time-based processes.

Shu-ying is currently pursuing her BFA in Media Art (Photography) at the Nanyang Technological University, School of Art, Design and Media.
