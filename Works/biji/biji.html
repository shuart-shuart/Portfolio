笔迹 script/ notes

2021-2022
Artist Book
Designed by Mingli Seet Published by THEBOOKSHOW as part of A Different Reading

笔迹 script/ notes looks at 18 years worth of hand-copied and hand-annotated scores from Shu's collection. Through observing and re-presenting these markings, she considers the way language and customs are developed in social groups and how it borrows and evolves from existing and adjacent communities.

笔迹 script/ notes was launched with THEBOOKSHOW during the Singapore Art Book Fair 2022, the book is available here.
