The Gallery

2017
Video Performance

Gallery explores the impossibility of a safe space boasted by commercial art spaces. It tests the endurance of a work of art. The painting is pitted against the forces of time and nature and the very definition of art itself. How much damage (or conservation and repair) can a piece of work be subjected to before it ceases to be a piece of art. The pursuit of a perfect space for an artwork is likely a farce.
