national flower
2020 - ongoing
Photo Series and Photobook

national flower takes an affectionate look at the bougainvillea plant and how things are in Singapore's landscape.

national flower was launched with THEBOOKSHOW during the Singapore Art Book Fair 2021, the book is now sold out. The work was also showcased in CICA Museum, Korea for Persepectives 2022 and DECK, Singapore Undescribed #7.
