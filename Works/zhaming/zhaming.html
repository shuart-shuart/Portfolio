乍鸣 creak of dawn
2024
Montage of self-recorded and found field recordings of birds at sunrise
30’42”

Audio can be found here.

What other signifiers of time are there beyond the standardised units of years, days, hours, minutes and seconds that we are so used to? I remember this phrase "日出而作 日落而息“ which translates to work when the sunrises, rest when the sunsets; I found it so elegant that this phrase from a folk song in the Yao period said so much about how in sync the people were with their environment then and the harmonious relationship they shared with nature. This work takes from field recordings and found recordings to create a cacophony of bird sounds as an alternative way to document and signal the start of a day.
