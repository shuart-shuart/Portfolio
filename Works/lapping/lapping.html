临摹 Lapping Emulations

2020-2021
Experimental Photography Prints on Awagami Paper, Fabric Projections, Mixed Media with Seaweed and Booklet

Lapping Emulations adopts a process-led investigation of the nuances and complexities of an unstable and protean cultural identity. Shu conjures new manners of image-making with her improvised darkroom and editing techniques. These improvisations shuffle across literal and abstract littoral imageries, articulating her rumination as part of Singapore’s Chinese diaspora. These intuitive processes are modulated by muscle memory from her formal photography training.

Lapping Emulations surfaces as a layering of tides. It voyages from the tangible to something more impressionistic, sentimental and introspective.

Lapping Emulations is Shu’s graduating project, it was shown at Gilman Barracks as part of ADM Show 2021. The project was selected for Inheritance, Objectif's Women in Film and Photography 2021 showcase. It was also featured in the Jakarta International Photography Festival's photobook showcase.
