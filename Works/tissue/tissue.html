Untitled (Tissues and Photos)

2019
Mixed Media Collage

Untitled (Tissues and Photos) was born out of an instinct-driven process of purging, revisiting and obscuring writings and photos of my father. Writing in Chinese is a way for me to slow down my thoughts; it is a language I speak and think in, but seldom write.
