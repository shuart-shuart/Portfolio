In the Aisle

2019
Photographic Series
(Wheat-pasted Prints and Zine)

In the Aisle gathers titbits of conversations and textures scavenged from Chinese Supermarkets. These supermarkets play crucial social roles for the Chinese communities in Edinburgh. Driven by the need to mine for social and emotional familiarity as a way of coping, I found myself comfortably nestled within sights and smells reminiscent of home. The most humble and banal conversations gave pulse to these spaces while the scents and scenes relieved me of being “the other” in this strange land.
